The Geography of Haiti

Haiti is a small Caribbean country located on the western part of the island of Hispaniola. It shares the island with the Dominican Republic, which occupies the eastern part. The country is divided into ten departments, each with its own distinct geography and culture. The capital, Port-au-Prince, is located on the west coast of the country, while the northern coast is lined with beautiful beaches that attract tourists from all over the world.
