Location of Mercedes Benz Stadium

The Mercedes Benz Stadium is located in the heart of downtown Atlanta, Georgia. It is situated at 1 AMB Drive NW, Atlanta, GA 30313. It is home to the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL) and Atlanta United FC of Major League Soccer (MLS).
