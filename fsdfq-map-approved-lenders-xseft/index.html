What is a MAP Approved Lender?

A MAP approved lender is a financial institution that has been approved by the government to participate in the Mortgage Assistance Program. MAP approved lenders are required to follow specific guidelines set forth by the government to ensure that they are providing fair and affordable loans to qualified individuals.

To qualify for the Mortgage Assistance Program, you must meet certain income and credit requirements. You must also be a first-time homebuyer, meaning you have not owned a home in the past three years.
