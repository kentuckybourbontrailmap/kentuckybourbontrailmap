The Adventure Begins

If you are a fan of Dora the Explorer, you must have watched Season 1 Episode 19. This episode is titled “The Map”. The episode starts with Dora and Boots on a beautiful day in the forest. They come across a map that was dropped by the wind. The map leads to a treasure, and Dora and Boots decide to follow it.

The map is not like any other map because it speaks and gives directions. It guides Dora and Boots through the forest, across the river, and over the mountains. Along the way, they meet with different obstacles, but they solve them using the help of the map.

The Obstacles

The first obstacle they encounter is a big river. There is no bridge to cross the river, and the current is too strong to swim. The map suggests that they use the stepping stones to cross the river. Dora and Boots use the stepping stones, and they cross the river safely.

The second obstacle is a mountain. The map guides them through a tunnel in the mountain. Inside the tunnel, they meet a bat who is lost. They offer to help the bat find its way out of the tunnel. They succeed, and the bat leads them to the other side of the mountain.

The Treasure

After crossing the mountain, Dora and Boots finally find the treasure. The treasure is a chest full of gold coins and precious jewels. Dora and Boots are very happy, and they thank the map for guiding them to the treasure.
