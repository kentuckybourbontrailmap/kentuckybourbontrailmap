Introduction

Are you planning your next outdoor adventure, but don’t want to get lost in the wilderness? Fear not, because OS map apps are here to help. With the rise of technology, you no longer need to carry around a cumbersome paper map. Instead, you can access a digital map on your smartphone or tablet, with features such as GPS tracking, route planning, and weather updates. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best OS map apps available in 2023, and how they can enhance your outdoor experience.

What is an OS Map App?

OS stands for Ordnance Survey, which is the national mapping agency for Great Britain. An OS map app is a digital version of the traditional paper map, which shows the topography, terrain, and landmarks of a particular area. OS maps are highly detailed, and include information such as footpaths, bridleways, and contours. The app can be downloaded onto a smartphone or tablet, and used to navigate while out and about.

Why Use an OS Map App?

Using an OS map app has several advantages over a paper map. Firstly, it’s much easier to carry around – you don’t have to worry about it getting wet or torn. Secondly, it’s more interactive – you can zoom in and out, and switch between different layers of information. Thirdly, it’s more accurate – GPS technology means you can pinpoint your exact location, and plan your route accordingly. Finally, it’s more up-to-date – the app can be regularly updated with new information and features.

The Best OS Map Apps in 2023

1. OS Maps

OS Maps is the official app from Ordnance Survey, and is widely regarded as one of the best OS map apps available. It offers access to all of the OS maps for Great Britain, as well as a range of features such as route planning, GPS tracking, and weather updates. The app is free to download, but you’ll need to pay for a subscription to access some of the more advanced features.

2. ViewRanger

ViewRanger is another popular OS map app, which offers a range of features for outdoor enthusiasts. It includes access to a range of maps, including OS maps, as well as route planning, GPS tracking, and augmented reality. The app is free to download, but you’ll need to pay for a subscription to access some of the more advanced features.

3. Komoot

Komoot is a slightly different take on the traditional OS map app, as it focuses more on route planning and navigation. It includes access to a range of cycling and hiking routes, as well as a route planner which takes into account factors such as distance, elevation, and surface type. The app is free to download, but you’ll need to pay for a subscription to access some of the more advanced features.

How to Use an OS Map App

Using an OS map app is relatively straightforward, but there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, make sure you download the app before you head out into the wilderness, as you may not have access to Wi-Fi or mobile data. Secondly, familiarize yourself with the different features of the app, such as route planning and GPS tracking. Thirdly, make sure your device is fully charged, or bring a power bank with you. Finally, don’t rely solely on the app – always carry a paper map and a compass as a backup.

Question & Answer

Q: Do I need mobile data or Wi-Fi to use an OS map app?

A: No, you can download the maps onto your device before you head out, and use GPS tracking without an internet connection.

Q: Can I use an OS map app for cycling as well as hiking?

A: Yes, many OS map apps include cycling routes as well as hiking routes, and allow you to plan your own routes based on factors such as distance and elevation.

Conclusion

OS map apps are a great way to enhance your outdoor experience, and ensure you don’t get lost in the wilderness. Whether you’re hiking, cycling, or exploring a new area, an OS map app can help you navigate with ease. By downloading one of the best OS map apps of 2023, such as OS Maps, ViewRanger, or Komoot, you’ll have access to a range of features such as route planning, GPS tracking, and weather updates. So what are you waiting for? Download an OS map app today, and start exploring the great outdoors!
