What are the Must-Visit Places in Java Island?

Java Island has a lot of beautiful places to visit, but some of the must-visit places are:

  • Borobudur Temple, the largest Buddhist temple in the world
  • Mount Bromo, an active volcano with a stunning sunrise view
  • Dieng Plateau, a highland area with a beautiful natural landscape
  • Prambanan Temple, a Hindu temple complex with unique architecture
  • Yogyakarta, a city with a rich cultural heritage and delicious culinary
