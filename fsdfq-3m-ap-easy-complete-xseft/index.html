What is 3M AP Easy Complete?

3M AP Easy Complete is a water filtration system that is designed to remove impurities such as chlorine, sediment, rust, and other contaminants. The system uses a combination of carbon block filtration and ion exchange technology to purify water. The product is easy to install and can be used for both residential and commercial purposes.

How does 3M AP Easy Complete work?

The 3M AP Easy Complete system uses a four-stage filtration process to remove impurities from water. The first stage involves a sediment filter that removes large particles such as sand and dirt. The second stage uses a carbon block filter that removes chlorine, bad taste, and odor. The third stage involves an ion exchange resin that removes heavy metals such as lead, mercury, and copper. The fourth and final stage uses a second carbon block filter to ensure that the water is free from any remaining contaminants.
