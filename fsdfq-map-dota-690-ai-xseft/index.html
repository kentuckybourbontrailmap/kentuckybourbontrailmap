What is Dota 6.90 AI?

DotA 6.90 AI is the latest map version of the Defense of the Ancients game. The map comes with several new features, including new heroes, items, gameplay mechanics, and bug fixes. The AI version also includes an improved artificial intelligence system, which enhances the game’s difficulty level.

What’s New in Dota 6.90 AI?

The new map version comes with several exciting new features, including:

New Heroes

Dota 6.90 AI introduces two new heroes – Void Spirit and Snapfire. Void Spirit is a melee hero with magical abilities, while Snapfire is a ranged hero with a unique skillset.

New Items

Gameplay Mechanics

Tips & Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to improve your gameplay in Dota 6.90 AI:

1. Focus on Farming

Farming is one of the essential aspects of the game. Ensure that you focus on farming to gain gold and experience points, which are essential for improving your hero’s abilities.

2. Master Your Hero’s Abilities

Each hero in the game comes with unique abilities. Master your hero’s abilities to maximize their potential in the game.

3. Communicate with Your Teammates

Communication is essential in any team-based game. Ensure that you communicate effectively with your teammates to coordinate your gameplay and achieve your objectives.

Neutral items can provide significant advantages in the game. Use them wisely to gain an edge over your opponents.

Controlling the map is an essential aspect of the game. Ensure that you control key areas and objectives to gain an advantage over your opponents.

Question & Answer

Q: What are Outposts?

Outposts are new structures introduced in Dota 6.90 AI. Players can capture outposts to gain experience points and provide a strategic advantage.

Q: What are Neutral Items?

Neutral items are new items that can be found in the jungle. They provide unique advantages to the player who picks them up.

Hero Kill Streak Gold is the bonus gold awarded to players who kill enemy heroes who are on a kill streak.
