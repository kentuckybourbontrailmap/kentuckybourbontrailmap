Introduction

Dreams Riviera Cancun Resort is a luxurious all-inclusive resort located in the heart of Cancun, Mexico. The resort boasts of a beautiful beachfront, stunning pools, and top-notch amenities that cater to families, couples, and solo travelers. Whether you’re planning a wedding, honeymoon, or a family vacation, the resort has something for everyone. In this article, we’ll explore the resort’s map, including its features, amenities, and other important details.

Location

The resort is located in Puerto Morelos, a charming fishing village that lies between Cancun and Playa del Carmen. The resort is easily accessible from the Cancun International Airport, which is about 20 miles away. The resort’s location offers easy access to popular attractions such as the Mayan ruins, cenotes, and other natural wonders.

Accommodation

The resort features 486 luxurious rooms and suites that are elegantly decorated and equipped with modern amenities. The rooms feature either a garden, pool, or ocean view, and they come with a private balcony or terrace. The rooms are categorized into various types, including Deluxe Garden View, Preferred Club Ocean View, and Honeymoon Suite, among others.

Dining

The resort offers an array of dining options, including international cuisine, seafood, Mexican, Italian, and Asian cuisine. The restaurants feature both indoor and outdoor dining options, and they are open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The resort also offers 24-hour room service for guests who prefer dining in the comfort of their rooms.

Activities

The resort offers a wide range of activities for guests of all ages. The resort features three swimming pools, one of which is an infinity pool that overlooks the ocean. The resort also offers non-motorized water sports such as kayaking, snorkeling, and paddleboarding. Other activities include beach volleyball, tennis, and yoga.

Entertainment

The resort offers nightly entertainment, including live music, shows, and performances. The resort also features a nightclub, where guests can dance the night away.

Spa

The resort features a world-class spa that offers a range of treatments, including massages, facials, and body treatments. The spa also features a hydrotherapy pool, sauna, and steam room.

Meeting and Events

The resort offers state-of-the-art meeting and event facilities that are perfect for weddings, conferences, and other events. The resort’s event planners can customize packages to suit your specific needs and requirements.

FAQs

Q: Is the resort all-inclusive?

A: Yes, the resort is all-inclusive, which means that all meals, drinks, and activities are included in the room rate.

Q: Is the resort family-friendly?

A: Yes, the resort is family-friendly, and it offers a range of activities and amenities for children, including a kids’ club, a children’s pool, and babysitting services.

Q: Does the resort offer transportation services?

A: Yes, the resort offers transportation services to and from the airport for an additional fee. The resort also offers shuttle services to popular attractions in the area.

Q: What is the check-in and check-out time?

A: The check-in time is 3:00 pm, and the check-out time is 12:00 pm.

Q: Is there a dress code for the restaurants?

A: Yes, some of the restaurants have a dress code, which requires guests to wear resort casual attire.

Conclusion

Dreams Riviera Cancun Resort is a luxurious all-inclusive resort that offers a range of amenities and activities for guests of all ages. The resort’s map showcases the various features and amenities that the resort has to offer, including its accommodation, dining, activities, entertainment, spa, and meeting and event facilities. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business event, Dreams Riviera Cancun Resort has everything you need for a memorable stay.
