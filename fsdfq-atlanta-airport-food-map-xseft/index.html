Discovering the Best Restaurants in Atlanta Airport

Flying can be stressful, but it doesn’t have to be when it comes to finding a delicious meal. Atlanta airport boasts a variety of restaurants, cafes, and bars, offering a wide range of cuisines to satisfy your cravings. To make your dining experience more enjoyable, we’ve put together the ultimate guide to the best eats at Atlanta airport.

What are the Best Restaurants in Atlanta Airport?

One of the best restaurants to try is One Flew South, located in Terminal E. This restaurant offers an upscale dining experience, featuring an eclectic menu that combines Southern and Asian flavors. If you’re looking for something more casual, head to The Varsity, located in Terminal C. This restaurant serves classic American fast food, including burgers, hot dogs, and fries.

What are the Best Cafes in Atlanta Airport?

If you’re in the mood for a quick coffee break, head to Cafe Intermezzo, located in Terminal B. This European-style cafe offers a variety of coffee drinks, pastries, and sandwiches. Another great option is the Atlanta Bread Company, located in Terminal A. This cafe serves freshly baked bread, pastries, and sandwiches.

What are the Best Bars in Atlanta Airport?

For a pre-flight cocktail or beer, check out the Gordon Biersch Brewery, located in Terminal A. This brewery serves a variety of beers, including their famous Hefeweizen. Another great option is the SweetWater Draft House, located in Terminal B. This bar offers a variety of local beers and ales, including their signature 420 Extra Pale Ale.

How to Find the Best Eats at Atlanta Airport

With so many restaurants and cafes to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to eat. To make the decision easier, use the airport’s food map. The map is available online and at the airport, and it shows the location of all the restaurants and cafes, as well as their menus.

What are the Best Vegetarian Options at Atlanta Airport?

If you’re a vegetarian, there are plenty of options to choose from. Try the Mediterranean Veggie Sandwich at Atlanta Bread Company or the Veggie Burger at The Varsity. One Flew South also offers a variety of vegetarian options, including the Grilled Tofu and the Vegetable Curry.

What are the Best Gluten-Free Options at Atlanta Airport?

If you’re gluten-free, don’t worry, there are plenty of options for you too. Try the Grilled Salmon at One Flew South or the Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad at Cafe Intermezzo. Gordon Biersch Brewery also offers a gluten-free beer, the Blonde Bock.

Tips for Enjoying Your Dining Experience at Atlanta Airport

To make your dining experience more enjoyable, arrive early at the airport. This will give you plenty of time to explore the different restaurants and cafes without feeling rushed. Also, make sure to check the hours of operation for each restaurant, as some may close earlier or later than others.

What are the Best Desserts at Atlanta Airport?

If you have a sweet tooth, you won’t be disappointed with the dessert options at Atlanta airport. Try the Cheesecake at Cafe Intermezzo or the Chocolate Chip Cookie at Atlanta Bread Company. One Flew South also offers a variety of desserts, including the Bourbon Pecan Pie and the Chocolate Torte.

What are the Best Late-Night Eats at Atlanta Airport?

If you have a late-night flight, don’t worry, there are still plenty of options for you. The Varsity is open 24 hours a day, serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Another great option is the TGI Fridays, located in Terminal B, which is open until midnight.

Conclusion

With so many options to choose from, dining at Atlanta airport can be a delicious experience. From upscale dining to fast food, there is something for every palate.
