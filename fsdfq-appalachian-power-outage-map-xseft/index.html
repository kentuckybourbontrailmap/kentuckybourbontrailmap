The Appalachian Power outage map provides customers with a wealth of information about the outage, including:

  • The cause of the outage

How Can Customers Use the Appalachian Power Outage Map?

Customers can use the Appalachian Power outage map to:

What are the Benefits of Using the Appalachian Power Outage Map?
