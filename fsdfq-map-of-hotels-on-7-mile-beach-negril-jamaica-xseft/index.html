What is 7 Mile Beach?

7 Mile Beach is a beautiful stretch of white sand beach that is located on the western coast of Jamaica. The beach is seven miles long, and it is known for its crystal clear water and beautiful sunsets. The beach is also home to many hotels, restaurants, and bars, making it the perfect place for tourists to stay.
